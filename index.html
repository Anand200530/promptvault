Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How much does it cost to sell prompts?
It's completely free to list and sell your prompts! We only take a 20% commission on each sale. You keep 80% of what you earn.
How do I get paid?
Earnings are deposited to your PayPal or bank account weekly. Minimum withdrawal is just $5, so you can cash out whenever you want.
What types of prompts can I sell?
Any AI prompts! ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and more. As long as they're original and useful, they're welcome here.
Can I offer free prompts too?
Absolutely! You can list both free and paid prompts. Free prompts are a great way to build your reputation and attract buyers to your paid offerings.
How does the preview feature work?
Buyers can test prompts with their own inputs before buying. This builds trust and reduces refunds, making your prompts more attractive.
Is my content protected?
Yes! All prompts are protected with digital rights management. Unauthorized sharing is detected and prevented.
